<h1>Aerodynamic analysis of wing and store separation interaction using CFD (Minor Project) </h1>

- Worked as a team of 3 to study the aerosynamic characteristics of store release on an airfoil in 2D.

- Used an overset dynamic mesh with a linear drop with UDFs.

- Submitted to the CFD Symposium 2026 conference awaiting response.

- Validated using a DTIC paper and a Masters thesis.
