<h1>2. Backward Step LES CFD</h1> 

- The primary goal of this project is to explore the use of LES turbulence modelling and learn the prerequisites to set up a LES simulation of the 2026 Hybrutos FSAE car using a GPU based HPC which is based on the Nvidia A100 to study the complex interactions of all the parts (This is a project in progress).
- The purpose of this project was to explore 3D turbulence in form of a backward step. <br> 
- A uniform mesh of 1mm was generated using ANSYS Meshing to resolve the kolmogorov scale enough to capture the small scale eddies. <br>
- A timestep size of 0.001 seconds was chosen and dues to a inlet velocity of 10m/s the cfl number acheived was 10. <br>
