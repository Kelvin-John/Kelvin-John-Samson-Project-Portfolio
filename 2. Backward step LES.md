<h1>2. Backward Step LES CFD</h1> 
- The puspose of this project was to explore 3D turbulence in form of a backward step. <br> 
- A uniform mesh of 1mm was generated using ANSYS Meshing to resolve the kolmogorov scale enough to capture the small scale eddies. <br>
- A timestep size of 0.001 seconds was chosen and dues to a inlet velocity of 10m/s the cfl number acheived was 10. <br>
